Gameplay

At the beginning of the battle both the player and the monster-enemy have 100 health points (HP).
The player chooses spell first. After that he faces the task screen.
If the task was solved correctly his spell will work. If not - the monster-enemy applies its spell on the player.
The winner is the one who remains alive and do not loses HPs.
If the player wins - the battle with the next monster-enemy begins.
If not - the score table will appear.
